Courses

We have Nursery, Junior KG, Senior KG and First available for your child.

Nursery

Nursery

Nursery typically refers to the first stage of early childhood education, usually for children aged around 2 to 4 years old. It serves as an introduction to formal schooling and focuses on basic socialization skills, early literacy, numeracy, and creative activities. In nursery, children learn through play, exploration, and hands-on experiences.

Juniour K G

Juniour K G

Junior KG is the next stage after nursery, typically designed for children aged around 4 to 5 years old. It builds upon the skills and concepts introduced in nursery and continues to focus on social, emotional, cognitive, and physical development. Children in Junior KG engage in more structured learning activities, preparing them for the transition to formal schooling.

Senior K G

Senior K G

Senior KG is the stage following Junior KG, also catering to children around 5 to 6 years old. It further refines the skills acquired in Junior KG and prepares children for entry into primary school or 1st Standard. Senior KG curriculum typically includes more advanced literacy and numeracy activities, as well as continued emphasis on social skills and creative expression.

First Standard

First Standard

1st Standard, also known as Grade 1 or Class 1 in some educational systems, marks the beginning of primary education. It is the first formal year of schooling and typically includes a more structured curriculum covering subjects such as language arts (reading, writing, and speaking), mathematics, science, social studies, and sometimes arts and physical education.
